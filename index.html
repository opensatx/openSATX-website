Sessions

Civic Hacking 101

New to civic hacking? Don't even know what that means? Every week, Christopher Whitaker leads quick workshop to get you up to speed. Or you can watch it online.

Intro to Programming

Many of our attendees are new to programming and want to learn. Every week Juan-Pablo Velez leads an informal session on how to use the command line and some basic principals of programming.
